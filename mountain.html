Description:

“The Mountain Is You” by Brianna Wiest is a nonfiction book that delves into the concept of self-sabotage and provides a comprehensive guide on how to overcome it. The book explores why people engage in self-destructive behaviors, when they do it, and most importantly, how to stop doing so for good.
