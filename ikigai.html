Description:

Ikigai is a concept in Japanese culture that refers to a person’s reason for living, a sense of purpose and fulfillment that brings happiness and satisfaction. The book “Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life” by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles explores this idea and its connection to longevity and well-being.
