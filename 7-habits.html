The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People, written by Stephen R. Covey, is a self-improvement book that presents a principle-centered approach to personal and interpersonal effectiveness. The book proposes that individuals can transform their lives by adopting seven habits, which are based on universal principles of human nature and enduring values.
